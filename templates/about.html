Why We Did It

When coming up with a theme, it was hard to not think of Covid-19 and its impact on the world’s populations. It got us to thinking about home, our population growth over the years and predictions that it’s on track to double by 2066. How is this happening? Is it the same in every country? Excluding immigration, where is this growth coming from? We wanted to know. So, like all good data nerds, we went looking for data and found the 2019 United Nations (UN) World Population Prospects, chock full of demographic indicators. We crunched it together and here’s our data viz “One Pager”.
