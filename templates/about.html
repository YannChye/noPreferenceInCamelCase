About Us

On the surface we’re a bit of an unusual match-up – an accountant, an emerging researcher and a teaching associate in neuroscience. Still, there’s one thing that makes us work and that’s our love of numbers. We all work with them and we really, really love them! That and did we mention we’re doing a Data Analytics bootcamp together?


Why We Did It

When we were asked to come up with a theme, with everything that’s been going on in 2020, it was hard to not think of Covid-19 and its devastating impact on the world’s population. This then got us to thinking about Australia’s historical population growth and ABS predictions in 2019 that we’re set to double our population by 2066. I mean, how is this happening? Is it the same in every country? Whilst immigration has and will no doubt continue to drive population growth in Australia, people are coming from somewhere. We wanted the know where. So, like all good data nerds, we went looking for data and found the 2019 United Nations (UN) World Population Prospects, chock full of demographic indicators. We crunched it together and here’s our data viz “One Pager”.


How We Did It

As with all data projects it starts with a huge amount of upfront planning and then quickly becomes about the tech. Boy have we used some tech! We started with our data source, a few data sets from the 2019 United Nations (UN) World Population Prospects, loaded them into Pandas and started crunching (yes, that is the technical term)
